The **MS Excel for Beginners Course** at **MSK Institute, Shikohabad** is designed to help students, working professionals, and beginners build a strong foundation in Microsoft Excel—one of the most important tools used in offices, businesses, schools, and data-related work. Excel is the backbone of data entry, reporting, analysis, and office productivity. Whether you are applying for jobs, looking to enhance your computer skills, or trying to manage personal or professional data, Excel is an essential skill that opens countless opportunities.

This beginner-friendly course teaches Excel from scratch, starting with the interface overview and gradually moving toward data entry, formatting, basic formulas, functions, and chart creation. Every topic is explained through practical examples, making it easy even for students with no prior computer knowledge. Our step-by-step approach ensures that learners gain confidence in using Excel efficiently for everyday tasks.

In this course, you will explore key Excel features such as **workbooks, worksheets, cell formatting, auto fill, simple formulas, SUM and AVERAGE functions, basic charts**, and much more. You will understand how to enter and edit data, apply number formats, adjust rows and columns, and perform simple calculations. These are the most used operations in offices, accounting firms, schools, coaching centers, and businesses.

The curriculum also includes a **Mini Project**, where learners create a complete **Student Report Card** using formulas, formatting, and charts—allowing them to apply everything they learned throughout the course. This practical exposure helps you build the skills required in real-world professional settings.

Whether you want to apply for office jobs, handle personal finances, maintain business records, or simply upgrade your computer knowledge, this course is the perfect starting point. With training designed by experienced instructors at MSK Institute, you will learn Excel in a simple, practical, and effective way.

By the end of this course, you will be able to navigate Excel confidently, prepare formatted sheets, use basic functions, create charts, print professionally formatted documents, and handle routine tasks independently.
